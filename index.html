ABOUT THE FACULTY

Welcome

The Faculty of Economics and Management Sciences was created to cater for the demands of stakeholders who are in favour of the departure from the traditional economics education and training. Our programs encompass all aspects of management related areas of economics, business administration, banking and finance, and accountancy as well as marketing.

With state-of-the-art learning environments and strong postgraduate programmes, the Faculty looks into a very bright future with great potentials of becoming a world class Faculty in the arena of Economics and Management Sciences.
